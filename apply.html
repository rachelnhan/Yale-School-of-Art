APPLICATION RESOURCES

About your application

The SoA admissions wiki provides key information for applying to Yale’s MFA program—use it as your main resource when preparing your application. For unanswered questions, contact the admissions office via email at art.admissions@yale.edu.

Note: Yale does not offer campus tours, portfolio reviews, or advice on application content. After submitting, check your application status and document receipt via the portal. Please avoid contacting faculty or students for program or application guidance to respect their time and privacy.
